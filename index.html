What To Bring

Camping Essentials
  • A tent with enough room for everyone, or friends you trust with tents
  • Sleeping bag or something to keep you warm at night
  • Folding tables, chairs
  • light-duty rope, twine
  • Cooler
  • Food and drink there are nearby stores, but you will be cooking stuff, or eating cold food
  • Trash bags
  • Bug spray / bug repellant
  • Flashlights or lantern
  • Extra batteries
  • Clothes
  • Swimsuit
  • A towel
  • Extension cords
  • FM radio so you can listen to main events and concerts
  • Sunscreen
Extra stuff
  • Portables (Switch, Zune, Oregon Trail, etc...)
  • Cell Phone
  • Camera
  • Extra money for goodies, pizza, emergencies, etc...
  • Plastic bins for washing dishes
  • Grill
  • Canopy
  • Consoles/Other Electronics At your own risk

Volunteer

Volunteeering for MAGStock is easy! Our events are built on volunteer participation, so we encourage every attendee to get involved.

Between prepping food or checking in attendees, there's something for everyone! We don't ask a lot, just signing up for one three hour shfit makes a world of difference to us. Volunteer for enough hours and you'll even be rewarded!

Volunteers are required to purchase admission to the event, but once done, you're off to the races! Make sure to check 'Sign me up!' when registering so we know you're interested. If you have any questions about, feel free to contact magstock@magstock.org.
